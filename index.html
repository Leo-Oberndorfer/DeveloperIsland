Coconut Coding

Coconut Coding

To make coding more fun we have our own competition system called "Coconut Coding" where you can compete against other developers and earn xp. The top 50 developers with the most xp will be displayed on the leaderboard.

XP

To earn xp you will have to complete challenges posted by our team members. The challenges have different difficulties which will range from 1 to 10. The higher the difficulty, the harder the challenge and the more xp you can earn. There are also some kind of requirements for each challenge which will give you different amount of xp when completed. All together they sum up to the maximum amount of obtainable xp.

Prize

To make the competition even more intense we give away prizes sometimes (Maybe once a month or so). We pick someone from the first page of the leaderboard and grant them their prize. I won't tell you what the prizes are, this would ruin the surprise ;)

Leaderboard

On our Leaderboard are the top 50 People with the most XP displayed.
