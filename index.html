Coconut Coding

XP

To earn xp you will have to complete challenges posted by our team members. The higher the difficulty, the more xp you can earn.

Challenges

The challenges have different difficulties which will range from 1 to 10. If you do more than the requirements say, you can get more Xp.

Leaderboard

On our Leaderboard are the top 50 People with the most XP and the highest level displayed.

Level

When you reach a certain amount of XP you get the respective Programming Level. For example, BEGINNER for the lowest and LEGEND for the highest.

Prize

To make the competition even more intense we give away prizes sometimes. We pick someone from the leaderboard and grant them their prize.

To make coding more fun we have our own competition system called "Coconut Coding" where you can compete against other developers and earn xp.
